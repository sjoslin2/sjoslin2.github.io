A Comparison of US Presidential Election Forecasting Methods

Instructions and Explanation

This site explore key US Presidential Election Forecasting Methods: what they are, their historical success rate, and how different methods compare for each election.

The first page, composed of 4 scenes, will focus on each forecasting method individually while the second page, and final scene, will allow you to free explore information regarding predictions for every election since 1936.

As you are exploring this Narrative Visualization, consider how election predictions are discussed and how these forecasting methods are never fully accurate.
